Our Background

Kasi Authentic eatery

Kasi Kulture was founded in 2023 with the idea of turning the old retro Kasi Kulture style into a new fresh and dynamic concept without compromising on the unique nostalgic feel and experience.

Kasi Kulture, a legendary spot in Kempton Park’s working-class neighbourhood of Birch Acres. Well known as a authentic eatery and has built a reputation for serving the most beloved and delicious bunny chow, famously known as kotas.

The canteen-style spot is loved for its mouth-watering and high-quality bunny chows, which have earned it a loyal following and a go-to spot for people looking to experience the true taste of this iconic type of South African food. What truly sets Kasi Kulture apart is its unwavering commitment to quality and flavour. Each bunny chow is a testament to the rich, traditional tastes of South African street food, prepared with a secret blend of spices and fresh, high-quality ingredients.

Although we ensure that our menu items are packed with flavour, we pride ourselves on providing value for money and our menu items are budget-friendly for the family. Whether you choose to sit in your car “old school “ style or make yourself comfy at a table on our deck areas, you will be ensured an incredible meal, friendly service with a smile, and vibey entertainment. Pull into Kulture Kasi to enjoy the unforgettable experience!

Mission

Our mission is to serve our community the most authentic and delicious bunny chows, using only the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes. We believe in providing more than just a meal; we want to create a warm and welcoming space where friends and family can gather to enjoy a taste of South African tradition. Our goal is to share our passion for high-quality, flavourful food while building lasting connections with every customer who walks through our doors. We are committed to maintaining the high standards that make our bunny chows so special, ensuring every bite is a memorable experience.

Vision

To become a beloved food spot recognized for preserving the tradition of the perfect kota, our vision is to be the leading destination for authentic South African bunny chow. We'll achieve this by focusing on exceptional quality and a welcoming environment. We aim to be more than just a restaurant; we want to be a cornerstone of the community, a place where our food brings people together and celebrates cultural heritage. We envision a future where the love for our unique and flavourful bunny chows’ spreads, creating a loyal customer base and a strong reputation for excellence that extends beyond our local neighbourhood, all while staying true to our roots and passion for sharing this iconic dish.
