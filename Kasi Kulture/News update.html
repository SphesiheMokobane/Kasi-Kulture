Our News update

Kasi Authentic eatery

We're excited to introduce Kasi Kulture, a new online spot for all things bunny chow. This isn't just a website; it's a place to celebrate the heart and soul of this beloved South African meal. Launched in 2025, our goal is to connect you with the best bunny chow makers in your area. We believe that good food is about community, and we hope this space helps you find your next favourite meal while supporting local spots that keep the tradition alive. Our platform makes it simple to discover hidden gems and browse menus from local takeaways. You can read genuine reviews from other bunny chow lovers and easily find a delicious meal near you. This is our way of celebrating the diverse flavours and unique recipes passed down through generations, ensuring the spirit of this iconic dish continues to thrive. Dankoo!
